MOOMOO'S

Moo Moo The Wine Bar and Grill is the home of mouth-watering steaks in Brisbane and on the Gold Coast. The steak restaurant and wine bar is an award winning restaurant delighting all who walk through the door.

Each venue offers a menu with 20 different cuts of steak to delight your tastebuds. While sensational beef and an expansive wine list are our passion, Moo Moo’s is more than a steak restaurant and wine bar.

Moo Moo offers fresh seafood, perfectly cooked poultry and delicious game that are perfectly paired with wines from all over the world. Beef is the star of the show at our steak restaurant and wine bar – our menus tell you the breed, age, size and diet of the cut you’re consuming. Specialty beef menu at our steak restaurant and wine bad offers some of the nation’s finest cuts, including pasture and grain fed, Wagyu and Angus breeds and of course our Signature in house 50 day dry aged beef, the richest and most luxurious steak you’ll find in Australia!

The menu at Moo Moo’s is constantly evolving with head chefs create new and exciting dishes and fine tune existing favourites. Although we are a steak restaurant and wine bar, Moo Moo’s prides ourselves on catering to all tastes and needs with menus clearly showing gluten free, Halal and vegetarian options.

Not only do we offer you the best steak restaurant and wine bar experience, the dessert menu at Moo Moo’s will have your sweet tooth tingling in anticipation. From start to finish, you will be satisfied with everything on offer at our steak restaurant and wine bar.
